Our story

Why we built Pickify Lab

Pickify Lab started as a simple frustration: short-form video is where most people actually discover skincare products now, but it's a terrible place to research them. A 30-second clip can't tell you whether a serum will break you out, whether the "dupe" is really equivalent, or whether the brand behind the viral claim has any research to back it up.

So we built a place to land after the video ends — a searchable, honest library of reviews covering the products people are actually asking about, written in plain language, with the pros and the cons both given equal space.

How we review

Our process

  1. 1

    We track what people are searching for

    Products trending across TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube shape our review queue.

  2. 2

    We study the formula

    Every review starts with the actual ingredient list — what it does, and who it suits.

  3. 3

    We weigh the real-world feedback

    We cross-reference verified buyer feedback against the claims, not just the star average.

  4. 4

    We publish the whole picture

    Pros, cons, who it's for, who should avoid it — every review ships with both sides.
